Our Story

Built on Handshakes, Not Contracts

MGS Communications was founded in 1996 right here in Albuquerque with a simple idea: give people honest, reliable communication and security solutions — and answer the phone when they call.

For 30 years, we've served construction crews, hotels, schools, event coordinators, security teams, and families across New Mexico. We're an authorized Kenwood and Motorola dealer, and we stand behind every product we sell and every system we install.

When you call MGS, you talk to a real person. When you drop off a radio, you get a loaner — free. When you need a repair, we give you a free estimate and often finish the same day. That's not a marketing pitch — it's just how we do business.

Our Journey

30 Years of Growth

From a small radio shop to Albuquerque's go-to for communications and security.

1996

Founded in Albuquerque

MGS Communications opens its doors on Carlisle Blvd, offering two-way radio sales and repair to local businesses.

2002

Authorized Dealer Status

Became an authorized Kenwood and Motorola dealer, bringing commercial-grade equipment to New Mexico businesses.

2010

Security Systems Launch

Expanded into CCTV cameras, access control, and alarm systems — offering complete security solutions alongside radios.

2020

Statewide Service

Grew to serve all of New Mexico with on-site installations, from Las Cruces to Santa Fe and everywhere in between.

2026

30 Years Strong

Celebrating three decades of local, family-owned service. Still answering the phone, still providing free loaners, still here for you.
